Starting from 2005, I've been involved in developing websites for handheld devices, and backends for those sites. We started with our own homebrewed CMS system, and gradually transitioned to use Grails framework. My duties initially consisted of creating new features for the CMS platform, and implementing customer sites. Later I have assumed more the role of a lead developer and platform architect. My responsibilities include investigating emerging technologies and superimposing them on our customer requirements, so that Conmio developers may better respond to current and future customer needs.

Key skill areas in this position include "standard" Java servlet programming with Spring Framework, Groovy programming and Grails plugin development in TDD style using jUnit and JSUnit, and BDD with JDave and easyb. I have also worked as the lead developer in a JavaScript-based RIA tablet/handheld application, implemented using Backbone.js and RequireJS, with a Grails+MongoDB backend. I was also responsible in transitioning the company codebase from SVN to Git, and associated developer training and best practices creation.

I regularly take part in customer negotiations to plan and oversee the implemented architecture, and also get a grassroots view of the customer's value foundation and business needs.
